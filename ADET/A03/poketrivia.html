Legendary Heritage

History

The Pokémon franchise began in 1996 with the release of Pocket Monsters Red and Green in Japan. What started as simple Game Boy games has evolved into one of the most successful entertainment franchises of all time, spanning games, cards, anime, movies, and merchandise loved by millions around the globe.
