ABOUT

Several thousand years after a mysterious phenomenon that turns all of humanity to stone, the extraordinarily intelligent, science-driven boy, Senku Ishigami, awakens.

Facing a world of stone and the total collapse of civilization, Senku makes up his mind to use science to rebuild the world. Starting with his super strong childhood friend Taiju Oki, who awakened at the same time, they will begin to rebuild civilization from nothing...

Depicting two million years of scientific history from the Stone Age to present day, the unprecedented crafting adventure story is about to begin!

Dr. Stone: Science Future Announces New Cast

The final season of the hit anime, "Dr. Stone: Science Future", is set to premiere in January 2025. The season will introduce new characters, Dr. Xeno and Stanley Snyder, voiced by Kenji Nojima and Kōji Yusa, respectively. These characters promise to make the last arc of the show more intense and intriguing.

The first season will debut at CCXP (Comic Con Experience) in Sao Paulo, Brazil, from December 5–8, 2024. Crunchyroll will stream the show globally, ensuring fans worldwide can witness the thrilling conclusion to Senku’s scientific journey.

A one-off anime episode, (Dr. Stone: Ryusui), further expanded the lore and world-building of the 'Dr. Stone' universe. The third season, 'Dr. Stone: New World', premiered in April 2023 and concluded its first cour after 11 episodes. The second cour aired in October 2023, with the series officially debuting in December 2023.

(Dr. Stone) manga, a collaborative creation by Riichirou Inagaki and Boichi, gained immense popularity during its run in 'Weekly Shonen Jump' until its conclusion in March 2022. It was translated into English by Viz Media and distributed digitally on MANGA Plus.

As the anime enters its final season, fans eagerly anticipate how 'Dr. Stone' will conclude its epic tale of scientific discovery, survival, and human creativity.
