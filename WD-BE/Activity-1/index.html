About realme

realme is a global technology brand for the young and by the young, established on May 4, 2018 by Sky Li.

Guided by its “Dare To Leap” spirit, the brand entered the Philippines in November 2018 and quickly disrupted the local market with a lineup of trendsetting yet competitive products that provide top-notch quality, innovative features, and only the best digital experiences for its users and fans.

By utilizing cutting-edge technologies on its smartphone and AIoT devices, realme carved its way to be the top-of-mind tech brand for the young and stylish Filipino. In 2022, realme Philippines adopted the global “SPIRE Strategy”, integrating one leap-forward technology into all of its new products, to be supported by three pillars: design, performance, and experience.

Since then, realme has become the #1 Smartphone Brand in the Philippines, now for two consecutive years, as backed by IDC and Canalys sell-in reports published 2021 & 2022. realme remains to be one of the fastest-growing smartphone brands in the world, with the Philippines as one of its key markets.
