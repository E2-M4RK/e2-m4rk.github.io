House of Shot Cafe: Laguna’s first sports cafe ☕️

Welcome to House of Shots Sports Cafe! As Laguna's first sports cafe, we're passionate about celebrating the thrill of sports while serving up the finest brews. Our commitment to quality shines through in every cup, featuring premium Arabica and Robusta beans, alongside our unique local blend, Cafe de Cabuyao, crafted to support local farmers.

At House of Shots, we believe that great coffee fuels great moments. That’s why we use top-notch Torani sauces and syrups to elevate your coffee experience. Whether you’re catching a game or unwinding after a workout, our menu also boasts delicious chicken wings, takoyaki, and pizza perfect for sharing with friends.

Join us at our cozy spot in Cabuyao, where Filipino hospitality meets the vibrant world of sports. Feel at home with us, and remember: "Be humble in victory and Give God all the glory." Discover your new favorite hangout at House of Shots Sports Cafe!
